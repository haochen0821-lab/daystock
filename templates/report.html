⚠ Year-End Tax Filing Reminder

Taiwan (TW) Stock Dividend Tax

  • Option A - Combined Filing: Stock dividends are combined with personal income and taxed at the progressive rate (5%~40%). A deductible credit of 8.5% of dividend income applies (max NT$80,000).
  • Option B - Separate Taxation (28%): Stock dividends are taxed separately at a flat 28% rate, not combined with other income. Suitable for high-income taxpayers.
  • Compare both options and choose the one that results in lower tax liability.

United States (US) Capital Gains Tax

  • Short-Term Capital Gains: Assets held for 1 year or less. Taxed as ordinary income (10%~37% depending on bracket). Check "Hold Days" column for trades under 365 days.
  • Long-Term Capital Gains: Assets held for more than 1 year. Preferential tax rates of 0%, 15%, or 20% depending on taxable income.
  • Wash sale rule: Cannot claim a loss if you repurchase the same or substantially identical security within 30 days before or after the sale.
  • Consider tax-loss harvesting before year-end to offset gains.

* This is a general reminder only. Please consult a qualified tax professional for your specific situation.
