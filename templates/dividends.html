US Dividend Tax Info

30% Withholding Tax applies to dividends paid by US companies to Taiwan residents.

As a non-resident alien (NRA) from Taiwan, the US imposes a flat 30% withholding tax on dividend income. Taiwan does not have a tax treaty with the US, so no reduced rate is available.

The tax is automatically deducted by your broker before the dividend is deposited into your account.

Example
Dividend declared: $100
Withholding (30%): -$30
You receive: $70
